Hi! I'm Huey Teng.

Copywriter and front-end enthusiast,
based in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia.

This is me, when I was doing a 2-year work, climb and travel adventure in Australia and New Zealand.

But before that, I was working with advertising agencies in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia as an English and Malay Copywriter for 7 years. Throughout those years, I have developed various campaigns, creative concepts and copy content for clients such as, Nestlé, Fraser & Neave, Sara Lee, Intel and New Straits Times.

After my Working Holiday stint in the land down under, I moved to Canada and have embarked on an entirely new (and rewarding!) journey as a full-time mom. However, even as a stay-home mom, I continued to seek for ways to upgrade myself, especially when it comes to the latest digital trends - my most recent endeavour being front-end development.
